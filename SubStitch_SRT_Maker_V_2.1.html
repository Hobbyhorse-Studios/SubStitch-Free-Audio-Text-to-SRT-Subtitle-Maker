SubStitch 

Free Audio & Text to SRT Subtitle Maker

Drop in an MP3 and the text someone reads in it. Get back a timed .srt you can ship. No accounts, no servers, no cost — the audio never leaves this tab.

Rough timing: estimates across the audio and snaps to detected pauses. Instant and fully offline, but not word-perfect — captions can drift by a word or few, especially in long stretches. Great for quick jobs and steady narration. Want exact timing? Try Precision →

With Sync to audio on, SubStitch reads the actual sound to find pauses, then pins your sentences to the biggest pauses (the natural breaks between sentences and slides) — so each sentence re-anchors to real audio instead of drifting. Punctuation (. ! ?) marks where sentences end. Without clear pauses, or with sync off, it falls back to estimating from word length. For very long pieces, splitting into sections still gives the tightest results.
